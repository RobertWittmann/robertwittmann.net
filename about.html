About

Good, you made it! I'm the titular Robert (pronounced raw-burt) and I consider myself a UX designer by trade, explorer by heart.

I've always loved exploring different creative media and trying to merge them into one another, whether physical or digital. It's also a passion I'm passionate about I love sharing with other - not just the product, but also the process. Working together to get somewhere I couldn't get alone through honesty, community, and a little bit of Mario Kart.

Now my path has led me here: doing what I can to make things as joyous for others as possible. Focusing on people to create products which are intuitive, accessible, and beautiful.
